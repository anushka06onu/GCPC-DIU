GCPC Wings

PR & Development Wing

Public Relations, Marketing, and Development initiatives to enhance GCPC's visibility, engagement, and growth.

Wing Focus Areas

Public Relations

Building and maintaining relationships with stakeholders, media, and the community to promote GCPC's mission and activities.

Marketing & Branding

Creating compelling content, managing social media presence, and developing marketing strategies to increase visibility.

Development & Growth

Planning and executing initiatives for organizational development, member engagement, and sustainable growth.

Key Activities

Content Creation

Developing engaging content for social media, newsletters, and promotional materials to showcase GCPC activities and achievements.

Event Promotion

Managing publicity for GCPC events, workshops, and seminars through various channels and partnerships.

Community Engagement

Building relationships with industry partners, alumni, and other student organizations for collaboration and support.

Member Recruitment

Developing strategies to attract and onboard new members, ensuring diverse and inclusive participation.

How to Join

Requirements

  • Passion for communication and marketing
  • Creative thinking and content creation skills
  • Interest in public relations and community building
  • Commitment to GCPC's mission and values

Application Process

  • Register interest through GCPC membership
  • Attend PR wing orientation session
  • Submit portfolio or creative samples
  • Complete wing-specific onboarding
