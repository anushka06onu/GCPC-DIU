Introduction to Research & Complete Guideline for Beginners

Organized by: Research Wing, DIU GCPC, Department of CSE

Duration: 2 Hours | Mode: Offline | Target: Beginner undergraduate students

This seminar is designed for students who are new to research and need a clear roadmap from topic selection to publication.

Instructor/Speaker: To be announced.

Key Coverage

  • Importance of research in academic and professional growth
  • Choosing a research topic and finding research gaps
  • Paper writing structure and publication workflow
  • Tools: Google Scholar, IEEE, Zotero and related resources

Expected Impact

  • Clear understanding of beginner-level research process
  • Higher motivation for publication-oriented work
  • Stronger engagement with GCPC Research Wing activities
