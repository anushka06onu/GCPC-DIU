About GCPC

Who We Are

Our Story

GCPC was founded to create a dedicated and supportive ecosystem where female CSE students can learn technology deeply, practice problem-solving, and build confidence through leadership and collaboration.

Since its formation, the club has hosted regular contests, seminars, workshops, and mentorship initiatives that have strengthened student impact across campus.

Our Mission

  • Encourage girls in programming and research
  • Build leadership and confidence
  • Promote a strong problem-solving culture
  • Support academic and career excellence

Our Vision

To create a strong community of women technologists who lead, innovate, and inspire.
