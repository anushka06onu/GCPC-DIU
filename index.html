About GCPC

Who We Are

History

GCPC was founded to build a dedicated environment where female students can develop technical depth, leadership capability, and professional confidence.

Mission

Encourage girls in programming and research, build leadership, and strengthen academic excellence.

Vision

Build a strong community of women technologists who lead, innovate, and inspire.

GCPC Wings

Structured Wings for Focused Impact

ACM Wing

Competitive programming growth, contests, algorithmic thinking, and technical intensity.

  • Programming contest training
  • ICPC preparation support

Research Wing

Research fundamentals to publication pipeline with guided mentorship and review sessions.

  • Research seminar series
  • Paper writing guidance
  • Project-to-publication roadmap

Career & Development Wing

Career-focused technical and soft-skill development through practical workshops and guidance.

  • Development training
  • Career seminars
  • Professional grooming
