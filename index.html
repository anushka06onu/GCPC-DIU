Girls Computer Programming Club (GCPC) is a student-led platform dedicated to empowering female students in programming, research, innovation, and leadership through semester-based workshops, seminars, and practical activities.

About GCPC

Who We Are

History

GCPC was founded to build an inclusive and high-impact technical ecosystem where female students can grow through practical learning and leadership.

Mission

Encourage girls in programming and research, build confidence, and establish a strong problem-solving and innovation-driven culture.

Vision

Create a strong community of women technologists who lead, innovate, and inspire across academia and industry.

GCPC Wings

Structured Wings for Focused Impact

ACM Wing

Competitive programming culture, algorithmic problem-solving, and contest-driven technical growth.

Research Wing

Research foundations, guided methodology, paper writing, publication readiness, and collaborative projects.

Career & Development Wing

Development training, professional skills, project practice, and career-oriented workshops.
