Research Wing

What This Wing Is About

Research Wing nurtures academic research culture inside GCPC. It supports students from research basics to structured paper writing and publication-readiness through mentoring and ongoing sessions.

How This Wing Helps

  • Builds research mindset and topic-selection ability
  • Improves literature review, methodology, and writing quality
  • Guides students toward conference/journal submission process
